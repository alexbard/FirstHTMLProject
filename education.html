Education

Education is one of the defining features of an individual, whether one chooses to pursue higher education or not will greatly change a persons life and will yield completely different personalities. An education can help a young person develop a healthy work ethic as well as provides them with a degree that allows them to get a decent career.

John English Middle School
The first school I attended was John English. Here, I made my first friends, some of which have stayed with me to this day. I also studied French in this school from the age of four. I am grateful that this school was in a good neighbourhood as children are very easily influenced at a young age and being raised in a bad neighbourhood could've drastically changed where I would've been today.
Richview Collegiate Institute
Richview is where I went to high school. I furthered my studying of French here for four more years in a French program. Most notably, I managed to achieve honour roll in this school for all four years and graduated with a certificate of proficiency in French.
Linux Course
During my break from school, I finished a course in Linux which was my first taste of the programming world. I ended up liking it and decided to go to school to become a programmer instead of going to schoolx to be a pilot.
Humber College
Here is where I am currently going to school to become a programming professional. Ideally, I will be able to get into a co-op program and career to make a living off of the degree that I earn here.
