About My Family and I

My immediate family consists of my father, my mother, my brother and my sister. I'll mainly be telling you about my parents because I've probably told you enough about myself already and my siblings share the same story as me having been born in Canada.

My Father

My father was born in the USSR in Minsk, Belarus on June 2 in 1957. His father was in the army so his family moved around a lot as he would have to serve in different countries. My father lived in Kazakhstan for most of his youth.

My Mother

My mother was born in the USSR in Kiev, Ukraine on February 13 in 1962. She had a very large family of five siblings and she lived there her whole life. My parents met in Kiev in 1990 when my father went there to find work after finishing his mandatory service in the military. In 1999, my parents finished their immigration process and arrived in Canada.
