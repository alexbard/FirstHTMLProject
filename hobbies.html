Hobbies

Hobbies contribute a very important part to someone's personality, they make it easier for an individual to seek out like minded people when engaging in conversation. If the individuals hobbies are tedious and require a lot of patience, these hobbies will make the person more patient when dealing with frustrating situations in life. I believe it would be ideal if people had hobbies that developed skills rather than hobbies that are meant to kill time.
